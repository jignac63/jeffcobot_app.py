Q: What are Jefferson County office hours?
A: Our offices are open from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Q: How can I get a marriage license?
A: You can get a marriage license from the Clerk and Recorder's Office. Visit jeffco.us for details.

Q: How do I report a road issue?
A: Use the “Report a Problem” tool on the Jefferson County website or call 303-271-5000.
