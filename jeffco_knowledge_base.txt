Q: What are Jefferson County's business hours?
A: Jefferson County offices are open from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Q: How can I apply for a marriage license?
A: You can apply for a marriage license at the Clerk and Recorder's office. Visit jeffco.us for more.

Q: How do I report a pothole?
A: Use the “Report a Problem” feature on the Jefferson County website or call 303-271-5000.
